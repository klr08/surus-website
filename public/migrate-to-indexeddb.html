Surus CMS - Storage Migration Tool

This tool will migrate your CMS data from localStorage to IndexedDB, which provides much more storage space (50MB+ vs 5MB).

Benefits of IndexedDB

  • Significantly more storage space (50MB+ vs 5MB)
  • No more "QuotaExceededError" when adding team members
  • Better performance for large datasets
  • More reliable storage that won't be cleared as easily
